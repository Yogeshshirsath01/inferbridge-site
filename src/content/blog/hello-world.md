InferBridge is live at [www.inferbridge.dev](https://www.inferbridge.dev).
Point your existing OpenAI SDK at our gateway and route across ten
providers — OpenAI, Anthropic, Together, Groq, DeepSeek, Cohere, Mistral,
Sarvam, Krutrim, and your own self-hosted models — with one API key.

This blog will document what we ship: routing decisions, cost
benchmarks, residency trade-offs, and the engineering behind the gateway.
